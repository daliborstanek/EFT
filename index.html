THE OPERATIONAL AREA

WELCOME TO NORVINSK

Escape from Tarkov is a hardcore and realistic online first-person action RPG/Simulator with MMO features and narrative-driven walkthrough. Players must escape the city of Tarkov, which has been sealed off by the UN and Russian military.

There are no respawns in a raid. If you die, you lose everything you brought with you (unless insured or in your secure container). This creates an intense atmosphere where every sound matters.

PMC vs SCAV

  • PMC (Main Character): Use your own gear. Gain XP. Complete quests. Progress your character. Risk: HIGH.
  • SCAV (Scavenger): Spawn with random gear. AI are neutral (usually). No risk to your own stash. Loot and transfer to PMC. Risk: LOW.

THE GAMEPLAY LOOP

01

PREPARE

Equip your PMC in the Main Menu (Stash). Choose weapons, armor, meds, and keys. Insure your gear.

02

INFILTRATE

Spawn into a map (Raid) with other players and AI. You have a time limit (usually 20-45 mins).

03

SCAVENGE & FIGHT

Loot containers, loose items, and dead bodies. Fight for survival against PMCs and Scavs.

04

EXTRACT

Reach a designated Extraction Point. If you timer runs out or you die, you lose your loot.

CRITICAL MECHANICS

BEGINNER INTEL

GEAR FEAR

Don't be afraid to lose gear. It sits in your stash doing nothing. Use it to survive and learn. Money comes and goes; experience is permanent.

MAP KNOWLEDGE

This is 90% of the game. Learn one map first (Customs or Ground Zero). Learn the spawns and extracts. Use a second monitor map (MapGenie).

AMMO MATTERS

Check "EFT Ammo Charts" online. Don't use ammo that can't penetrate Class 3 or 4 armor for PvP.

TRUST NO ONE

If it moves, it kills. VOIP can be friendly, but betrayal is part of the game.
